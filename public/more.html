Brief History

D'roid Technologies began in late september 2015 with the desire to bring about Technological change in Nigeria and the African Continent as a whole. D'roid started as just a simple idea with the main aim of improving the Technological standard of of Nigerians. In mid 2016, D'roid got its first real job in Web development and with that other jobs in the same area opened up.

In the year 2017, D'roid got other jobs in areas like software Development, Graphic designs and also areas in A+. Also in this year, we partnered with an organisation known as "School of Creativity" to host the first ever ICT Competition in Calabar which was called "MERIDIAN ICT COMPETITION". Since then, our companies have hosted the Competition every year till date.

In the year 2018, we met with a company and spoke to them about partering in order to develop "Nigeria's first ever Home Automatic security doors" using solar as our source of power. In mid of 2018, the two companies could'nt reach a steady position and therefore had to go their seperate ways. In 2019, we expanded our Total number of clients by 3 and handled about 6 public functions, gradually we are building a brand that one day would help humanity technologically..
